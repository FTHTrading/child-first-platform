# IMPACT CLAIMS PROOF MAP
**Platform:** Child First Platform
**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2026-04-02

---

## PURPOSE

This document maps every type of impact claim a Campaign Operator might make to its required evidence chain. It is used by Campaign Auditors, Verifiers, and the platform's content review process. A claim not listed here requires Platform Owner + Campaign Auditor determination before it may be published.

---

## HOW TO USE THIS MAP

1. Campaign Operator selects the type of impact they are claiming
2. Platform displays the corresponding evidence requirements (from this map)
3. Operator submits evidence at the time of the impact report
4. Verifier cross-references submitted evidence against this map
5. Verifier states in their report which items from the map were satisfied
6. Platform applies the appropriate claim state (Claimed / Verified / Disputed)

---

## IMPACT TYPE 1 — MEALS PROVIDED

### Claim: "X meals were provided to children"

**Minimum evidence for "Claimed" state:**
- Written impact report stating: number of meals, date range, distribution location
- No verification required to publish as "Claimed"

**Evidence required to upgrade to "Verified":**
| Evidence Item | Notes |
|---|---|
| Food purchase invoices | Show quantities purchased that match claimed meal count |
| Supplier delivery receipts | Confirm food was delivered to distribution point |
| Distribution attendance log | Anonymized head counts or contact counts per service date |
| Distribution site confirmation | Site exists and was operational during the claimed period |

**Verification standard:** At least 3 of the 4 items above, including the invoices. Head count alone is insufficient.

**Common problems that block verification:**
- "We ordered bulk food at the start of the month" — does not prove specific distribution
- Photos of food without purchase documentation
- Claiming a number based on site capacity rather than actual attendance

## IMPACT TYPE 2 — CHILDREN REACHED

### Claim: "X children received support"

**Minimum evidence for "Claimed" state:**
- Written statement of count and methodology
- Note: this claim has a higher public skepticism risk — Claimed state will display an explanatory note

**Evidence required for "Verified":**
| Evidence Item | Notes |
|---|---|
| Program enrollment records | Anonymized roster count from school, shelter, or food program |
| Service delivery log | Per-visit anonymized count records for the claim period |
| Partner org attestation | Written statement from a recognized partner confirming their count methodology |
| Statistical methodology | If using estimation: full methodology with data points must be documented |

**Verification note:** Counts verified here are independent of beneficiary records held by the CPO. Verifiers check counts against distribution systems, not against the platform's internal beneficiary records.

---

## IMPACT TYPE 3 — SPECIFIC GOODS DELIVERED (non-food)

### Claim: "X units of [clothing/supplies/school materials/hygiene kits] were distributed"

**Evidence required for "Claimed":** Self-reported quantity and description.

**Evidence required for "Verified":**
| Evidence Item | Notes |
|---|---|
| Procurement records | Purchase orders or invoices for the specific goods |
| Delivery receipts | Goods delivered to distribution location |
| Distribution manifests | Sign-off or batch count at point of distribution |
| Photos of goods (anonymized) | Images of goods; no identifiable children |

---

## IMPACT TYPE 4 — FINANCIAL AID DISTRIBUTED

### Claim: "$X was distributed directly to families in need"

This is a high-scrutiny claim type. "Verified" requires higher bar than other types.

**Evidence required for "Claimed":** Operator self-reports total distributed.

**Evidence required for "Verified":**
| Evidence Item | Notes |
|---|---|
| Individual transfer records | Anonymized receipts for each transfer (no family names shown to verifier) |
| Guardian consent documentation | Evidence that recipients consented to receiving aid |
| Bank statement or transfer log | Showing transfers out matching the claimed amount and time period |
| Third-party accounting confirmation | Preferred for amounts over $10,000 |

**Restriction:** Direct cash transfers to families face additional AML scrutiny. Amounts above a threshold require additional documentation. This claim type is only available to operators who have completed enhanced vetting.

---

## IMPACT TYPE 5 — PROGRAM ENROLLMENT / SERVICES CONNECTED

### Claim: "X children were enrolled in [school meal program / housing program / health services]"

**Evidence required for "Claimed":** Count and program type stated.

**Evidence required for "Verified":**
| Evidence Item | Notes |
|---|---|
| Enrollment confirmation | Official documentation from the program administrator |
| Program administrator contact | Verifier must be able to contact the program to confirm aggregate enrollment |
| Date range confirmation | Enrollment period matches the campaign period |

---

## IMPACT TYPE 6 — EMERGENCY AID RESPONSE

### Claim: "We provided emergency assistance to X children following [event]"

This is a time-sensitive claim type. Evidence may be submitted retrospectively within 30 days.

**Evidence required for "Claimed":** Narrative report filed within 48 hours of the event.

**Evidence required for "Verified":**
| Evidence Item | Notes |
|---|---|
| Incident documentation | News reports, government emergency declarations, or partner org reports confirming the event |
| Response confirmation | Distribution records or partner attestation that aid was delivered to affected children |
| Count methodology | How the count was determined (site capacity, sign-in, partner estimate with methodology) |

---

## IMPACT TYPE 7 — LONG-TERM PROGRAM OUTCOMES

### Claim: "X children experienced [reduced food insecurity / improved nutrition / stayed in school]"

This is the highest-scrutiny claim type. Requires extended evidence.

**Evidence required for "Claimed":** Statement with methodology disclosed.

**Evidence required for "Verified":**
| Evidence Item | Notes |
|---|---|
| Baseline measurement | Pre-program data showing starting state |
| Outcome measurement | Post-program data from same population |
| Measurement methodology | How data was collected; who collected it |
| Third-party review | External organization reviews the methodology and results |
| Statistical significance | If quantitative: confidence interval and sample size stated |

**Note:** This claim type requires Independent Director approval before being featured in platform-level marketing.

---

## EVIDENCE INTEGRITY REQUIREMENTS (ALL TYPES)

Regardless of claim type, all submitted evidence must:

1. **Be timestamped** — documents without dates are insufficient
2. **Match the claim period** — evidence from a different time period does not verify the current claim
3. **Be unaltered** — digitally altered documents invalidate the entire submission
4. **Be directly related** — evidence from a related but different program cannot be used
5. **Be submitted before the claim is published as "Verified"** — retroactive evidence is not accepted for post-hoc verification upgrades (new submission required)

---

## CLAIM ESCALATION

If a Verifier cannot confirm evidence after 14 days of requesting documentation:
- Claim is moved to "Claimed — Documentation Pending"
- Operator is notified: 30 days to complete documentation or claim is retracted
- If claim is retracted: the funded disbursement associated with the claim is flagged for audit review

If evidence is found to be falsified:
- Claim is immediately moved to "Retracted"
- Campaign is suspended pending investigation
- Disbursement trigger associated with the false claim is escalated to Platform Owner + Independent Director
- Clawback proceedings may be initiated
